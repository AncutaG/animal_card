Corgi, a " big " dog in a small body

The Welshmen, who developed my breed came to call it "dwarf dog" due to my long, thick body set on short, stuby legs(my legs are really called stumps).

  • Personality : Smart and alert, affectionate but not pushy, bold but kindly
  • My coat : Prepare yourself and your house, I shed...a lot, but I just need an easy maintenance during my life
  • Fun fact about me : I don't sit, I sploot and don't be amazed when you'll have the impressio that I fly
  • Popularity : The Queen of England is one of my biggest fans

Originally from Wales, the Pembroke Welsh Corgis are the oldest herding breed. These medium-sized and medium-energy dogs can grow between 25-27 pounds and live an average of 11-13 years. The breed is classified by the American Kennel Club as a member of the Herding group. Don't be scared if you will be herded too, just choose to love me as I am.
